Refining Capacity & Investments:

  • Since its establishment in February 2013, Wudi Refinery has fortified its position with a registered capital of 100 million yuan, spanning an expansive area of 2020 mu.

  • Achieved a significant milestone in 2016 by securing a 2.4 million tons/year non-state trade imported crude oil use quota. Committed investments totaling 4.3 billion yuan in 2016 towards our transformative "clean oil processing project."

  • By the end of 2019, Wudi Refinery achieved a major milestone with a completed investment of 3.8 billion yuan in this groundbreaking project. Our advanced refining infrastructure includes cutting-edge units such as heavy-duty asphalt, delayed coking, and gas oil hydrogenation, among others.

  • Refined Products List:
